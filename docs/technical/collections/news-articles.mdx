# News Articles Collection

The News Articles collection stores curated news content sourced from external feeds and editorial input, connected to jurisdictions and services for targeted distribution.

## Configuration

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Slug** | `news-articles` |
| **Admin Group** | News |

## Fields

| Field | Type | Required | Default | Admin | Description |
|-------|------|----------|---------|-------|-------------|
| `title` | text | Yes | — | — | Article headline |
| `slug` | text | Yes | — | Indexed | URL-friendly identifier |
| `source` | text | No | — | — | Source publication name |
| `sourceUrl` | text | No | — | — | Original article URL |
| `summary` | textarea | No | — | — | Brief article summary |
| `content` | richText | No | — | — | Full article content (Lexical editor) |
| `jurisdiction` | relationship | No | — | — | Source jurisdiction (single, to `jurisdictions`) |
| `services` | relationship | No | — | hasMany | Related services |
| `industry` | text | No | — | — | Industry sector tag |
| `publishedAt` | date | No | — | — | Original publication date |
| `minifluxId` | number | No | — | — | Miniflux feed reader entry ID |
| `targetJurisdictions` | relationship | No | — | Sidebar | Target jurisdictions for display |
| `targetLanguages` | select (hasMany) | No | — | Sidebar | Target languages |
| `scheduledPublishAt` | date | No | — | Sidebar, Indexed | Scheduled publication date |
| `expiresAt` | date | No | — | Sidebar, Indexed | Content expiration date |

## Dual Jurisdiction Fields

News articles have two jurisdiction-related fields serving different purposes:

1. **`jurisdiction`** (single relationship) — The source jurisdiction where the news originated. This is a single relationship to the `jurisdictions` collection.

2. **`targetJurisdictions`** (hasMany relationship) — The jurisdictions where this article should be displayed. This is the standard reusable field used for content filtering.

## Relationships

| Collection | Field | Direction |
|-----------|-------|-----------|
| Jurisdictions | `jurisdiction` | Article → Jurisdiction (source) |
| Jurisdictions | `targetJurisdictions` | Article → Jurisdictions (display targets) |
| Services | `services` | Article → Services (hasMany) |
| OpportunityBriefs | `newsArticle` | Brief → Article |
| Campaigns | `relatedContent` | Campaign → Article (polymorphic) |

## Search Integration

News articles are indexed in MeiliSearch under the `news` index. See [Search Library](../libraries/search) for configuration.

## Frontend Route

- **Listing:** `/[locale]/news` — Displays news articles filtered by the current jurisdiction

## API Endpoints

| Method | Endpoint | Description |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| GET | `/api/news-articles` | List news articles with optional filtering |
| POST | `/api/news-articles` | Create a new article |
| GET | `/api/news-articles/:id` | Get a specific article |
| PATCH | `/api/news-articles/:id` | Update an article |
| DELETE | `/api/news-articles/:id` | Delete an article |
